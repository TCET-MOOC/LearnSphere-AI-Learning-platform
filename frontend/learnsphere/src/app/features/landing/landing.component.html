FAQ
Common Questions
How do I know if my college is registered?
During registration, enter your college email address. The platform automatically checks if your college domain is registered and instantly unlocks free access if it is.
My college isn't registered — can I still use LearnSphere?
Yes. You can purchase any individual course as an external student. Prices start from ₹199 and you get lifetime access plus a certificate on completion.
Will my college accept the remedial certificate?
Only if your college has assigned you the remedial course via LearnSphere. If they assigned it here, the certificate is automatically sent to their system on your pass.
